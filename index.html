About Us.

S S Developers is a relatively new player in the residential and commercial building construction industry, having been founded in 2019. However, our CEO,Mr. Bhadresh Patel, has over 15 years of experience in the field, which has helped the company quickly establish itself as a reputable and reliable builder.

  • One of the standout features of S S Developers is their focus on quality construction.
  • From the materials they use to the workmanship they employ, the company is committed to delivering projects that not only look great but also stand the test of time.
  • This is evident in their projects, such as Saral Sky, Shyam Saral, and Saral Casa, which offer modern and luxurious living spaces for residents.

Saral Sky, for example, is a residential complex that features apartments with spacious layouts, premium fittings, and top-notch amenities. The project is located in a prime area of the city, making it convenient for residents to access schools, hospitals, and shopping centers. Similarly, Shyam Saral &Saral Casa are projects that caters to the needs of those looking for affordable yet stylish homes.
